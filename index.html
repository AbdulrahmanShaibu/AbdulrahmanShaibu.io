Mission

Our mission is to deliver unparalleled English language education that equips students with comprehensive skills and cultural insights necessary to excel in their academic, professional, and personal endeavors. We are committed to fostering a deep appreciation for the language, empowering students to communicate confidently and effectively in diverse contexts, and inspiring them to become lifelong learners who can navigate the global landscape with linguistic proficiency and cultural sensitivity.

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized globally as a leading provider of English language education, renowned for our innovative teaching methods, exceptional outcomes, and unwavering commitment to student success. We aspire to set the standard for excellence in language instruction, cultivating a learning environment that inspires, challenges, and empowers students to reach their full potential.

Our History

Luminous English Class was established in 2014 in Jangombe, Zanzibar, with a clear vision to elevate the standards of English language education. Since its inception, the institution has been dedicated to delivering high-quality instruction to students of all levels. Through a steadfast commitment to educational excellence and a passion for fostering linguistic mastery, Luminous English Class has rapidly evolved into a respected and influential institution. Our innovative teaching methodologies and student-centered approach have garnered widespread recognition, establishing us as a leader in English language education. Today, we continue to build on this legacy, striving to inspire and empower our students to achieve their fullest potential in a globalized world.

Frequently Asked Questions


What courses are available at Luminous English Class?

We offer a wide range of courses including grammar, phonology, writing skills, and more. Check out our course section for more details.

How can I enroll in a course?

You can enroll through our website by selecting the desired course and following the enrollment instructions.

What are the class timings?

Our classes are scheduled at various times throughout the day to accommodate different time zones. Please refer to the schedule on our website for specific timings.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling?

Most of our courses do not require any prerequisites. However, advanced courses may require some prior knowledge or experience. Please check the course details for specific requirements.

Do you offer any certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide certificates for successful completion of our courses. These certificates can be a valuable addition to your professional portfolio.

We offer a refund policy under certain conditions. Please refer to our refund policy on the website for more details.
